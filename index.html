Welcome to Lilith Scanlations!

Lilith is a non-profit group working on BL (Boys Love) manga, manhwa, and manhua. We do not accept donations or payments, so please support the creators by purchasing the raws on the official platforms, such as Ridibooks, Bomtoon, Bookcube, Peanutoon, Lezhin, etc. You can learn about creating an account and getting adult access at some of these sites using the Buyer's Tutorial link in the above menu. If you have questions or the site you'd like to purchase from is not available there, you're welcome to ask us in our Discord server and we'll do our best to guide you!

The best way you can support our team is to join us!!! Refer to the recruitment page above for details. We really need Cleaner/Redrawers and Korean, Japanese Translators at this time~~

If you're unsure of something, you can refer to the frequently asked questions. If you see a bug or have issues with performance of the site, please reach out to us on Discord. We appreciate these reports.

Before You Proceed

  • DO NOT share this website or any projects, chapters or screenshots containing unofficial translations publicly. Especially on any social media platforms such as TikTok, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, etc.
  • DO NOT engage with the publisher, author or artist unless you have purchased from the official sources and do not reference any material taken from the unofficial translations.
